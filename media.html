Do you have any questions?

1. What types of events do you cover with your video and audio capturing services?

We cover a wide range of events, including corporate events (conferences, seminars, workshops, and product launches), weddings and engagements, live performances (concerts, theater productions, and dance recitals), sports events, and personal celebrations (birthdays, anniversaries, and reunions). If you have a unique event that doesn't fit into these categories, feel free to contact us to discuss your specific needs.

2. How far in advance should I book your services for my event?

We recommend booking our services as early as possible to ensure availability on your preferred date. Ideally, you should reach out to us at least 3 to 6 months in advance, especially during peak seasons or for large-scale events. However, we understand that sometimes events are planned on short notice, and we will do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

3. What equipment do you use for video and audio capturing?

We use state-of-the-art equipment, including 4K and 8K cameras, high-definition audio recorders, and advanced editing software, ensuring crisp, clear, and vivid results. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest industry developments and regularly updates our equipment to maintain the highest quality standards.

4. How long will it take to receive the final video and audio files after my event?

The time required to deliver the final video and audio files depends on the complexity and duration of your event, as well as the level of post-production work involved. Typically, we deliver the final product within 4 to 6 weeks after the event. We will provide you with a more accurate timeline during our initial consultation.

5. Do you offer live streaming services for events?

Yes, we offer live streaming services for events upon request. This allows your remote audience to experience your event in real-time, ensuring that no one misses out on the action. Please contact us to discuss your live streaming needs and receive a personalized quote.

6. Can I request specific shots or scenes to be included in my event video?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to share their vision and preferences with our team to ensure that we capture the moments that matter most to you. During our pre-event consultation, we will discuss your specific requests and incorporate them into our shooting plan.

7. What are your pricing packages, and do you offer custom packages?

We offer flexible pricing packages to accommodate a range of budgets, ensuring that our high-quality video and audio capturing services are accessible to all. Our packages vary based on the event type, duration, number of cameras, and post-production requirements. We also offer custom packages tailored to your unique needs. Please contact us to discuss your requirements and receive a personalized quote.

8. Can I review the footage before the final edit?

Yes, we provide clients with the opportunity to review the raw footage before the final edit is completed. This allows you to provide feedback and request any necessary changes to ensure that the final product meets your expectations. Note that additional charges may apply for extensive revisions beyond the initial agreement.
